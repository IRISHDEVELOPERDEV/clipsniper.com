ClipSniper Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

At ClipSniper.com, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data you provide when logging in with TikTok (username, profile info, video analytics) to help you manage your content. We do not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your TikTok data only to:

  • Display your videos and analytics
  • Help you repurpose and schedule content
  • Improve our service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your data is stored securely and deleted upon request.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke access anytime via TikTok settings. Email us at support@clipsniper.com to request data deletion.

5. Security

We use HTTPS and secure OAuth to protect your data during transmission.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of ClipSniper means you accept the changes.
